Airline lost my luggage


...the intercom announces something in German.
I hear NOTHING.


...fast forward to the roughest airport luggage check-in and boarding EVEEEER where my luggage is taken to be loaded in another aircraft, I make it to Munich where I take the connecting flight to Berlin; my final destination.

As everyone is collecting their luggage, I notice mine hasn't popped up. Well, what's new?! The intercom announces something in German. I hear nothing. So I do what any normal human being would do...follow the crowd with the missing luggage.
